What Does the Assignment Calculator Do?

  • Helps you finish your assignment on time and on target
  • Gives you an action plan with target due dates
  • Outlines the process of researching and writing step by step
  • Provides guidance for each step of the process

Remember: Each project is different, so if you need more help with your research, AskUs.

Based on the University of Minnesota Libraries' Assignment Calculator.
